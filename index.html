Legal literacy is a necessity

This is a gamefied platform for legal literacy and awareness amongst children

Challenge

The lack of legal literacy and awareness among children in India is concerning their fundamental rights, specifically within the context of children's rights. This critical knowledge gap poses a substantial risk to their ability to advocate for themselves and leaves them susceptible to exploitation, neglect, and systemic injustices. Despite the existence of international conventions and national legislation safeguarding the rights of children, there is a profound disconnect between legal provisions and the actual awareness and understanding of these rights among the child population in India. This deficiency perpetuates a cycle of vulnerability and hinders the potential for a just and equitable society

Solution

Promoting legal literacy among children is essential to help them understand their rights and responsibilities, make informed decisions, and become responsible citizens. Here are some solutions to enhance children's legal literacy:

  • School Curriculum Integration
  • Interactive Workshop and Seminars
  • Legal Literacy Programs
  • Online Resources
  • Legal Aid Clinics
  • Legal Storytelling
